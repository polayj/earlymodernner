# Training Corpus

EarlyModernNER was trained on a diverse collection of Early Modern English texts (c. 1500-1800) from multiple sources.

## Primary Sources

### Old Bailey Online
Court proceedings from the Old Bailey, London's central criminal court. These documents contain rich mentions of people, places, and goods involved in legal cases.

- **Source:** [Old Bailey Online](https://www.oldbaileyonline.org/)
- **Period:** 1674-1913 (Early Modern subset used)

### Parsed Corpus of Early English Correspondence 2 (PCEEC2)
A collection of personal letters from the Early Modern period, providing naturalistic language use and personal names.

- **Source:** [PCEEC2 on GitHub](https://github.com/beatrice57/pceec2)
- **Period:** 1410-1695

### The Royal Society Corpus (RSC) 2.0
Scientific writings from the Royal Society, containing technical terminology, place names, and scholarly references.

- **Source:** [RSC at CLARIN](https://fedora.clarin-d.uni-saarland.de/rsc/)
- **Period:** 1665-1996 (Early Modern subset used)

### Trade Documents (EEBO & Archive.org)
A curated collection of trade-related documents including plantation records, commercial correspondence, colonial administration documents, and economic treatises. These texts are particularly rich in commodity mentions and Caribbean/Atlantic world toponyms.

- **Sources:** Early English Books Online (EEBO), Archive.org
- **Period:** 1614-1807

## Annotation Process

1. **Silver-standard annotation:** Documents were initially annotated using GPT-4 with specialized prompts for each entity type
2. **Human review:** Annotations were reviewed and corrected using custom review tools
3. **Blocklist filtering:** Common false positives were identified and filtered using entity-type-specific blocklists
4. **Synthetic augmentation:** Hard negative examples were generated to improve precision

## Entity Distribution

The corpus contains annotations for four entity types:
- **TOPONYM:** Place names, geographic features
- **PERSON:** Individual people with names
- **ORGANIZATION:** Named institutions and bodies
- **COMMODITY:** Trade goods, materials, foodstuffs

See the individual adapter READMEs for performance metrics on each entity type.
